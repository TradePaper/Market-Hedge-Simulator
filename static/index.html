What this model includes and excludes ▼
What this includes
  • Monte Carlo simulation with configurable paths and seeded RNG
  • Sportsbook P&L based on American odds (favourite and underdog)
  • NO-contract hedge leg priced at your true win probability estimate
  • Stochastic order fill — hedge may not execute at target price
  • Slippage cost on hedge entry (market impact)
  • Transaction fees charged as basis points on hedge notional
  • Latency penalty applied as additional adverse price movement
  • Analytical break-even win rate for the configured strategy
What this excludes
  • Real-time market data — model uses your inputs as fair value
  • Order book depth and market impact beyond slippage estimate
  • Correlation between sportsbook line movements and market prices
  • Regulatory, counterparty, and platform risk
  • Tax treatment of gains and losses
  • Margin requirements or collateral costs
  • Multi-leg or compound hedging strategies
  • The hedge uses a simplified single-price approximation
